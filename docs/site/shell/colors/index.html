    Colors

    Easybasic shell supports all the color of gwbasic. It have total 100 colors:

    • 24 text mood colors
    • 12 CGA mood colors
    • 64 EGA mood colors

    Text Mood¶

    Text mood have 8 background and 16 foreground colors. No monochrome color support.
